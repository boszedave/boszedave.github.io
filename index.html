Privacy Policy for Radiova

Introduction

Welcome to Radiova ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you understand how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our radio streaming application.

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use Radiova, a mobile application that allows you to discover and stream radio stations from around the world.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Favorites: We store your favorite radio stations locally on your device to provide personalized recommendations.
  • Preferences: Your app settings and preferences are stored locally on your device.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: We collect basic device information such as device type, operating system version, and app version to ensure compatibility and provide support.
  • Location Data: With your explicit permission, we collect your approximate location to:
    • Show nearby radio stations on the map
    • Provide location-based station recommendations
    • Display your current location on the interactive map

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect additional information:

Google AdMob

  • Purpose: To display advertisements in the free version of our app
  • Data Collected: Ad interaction data, device identifiers, and general location information
  • Privacy Policy: Google AdMob Privacy Policy

RevenueCat

  • Purpose: To process premium subscriptions and manage billing
  • Data Collected: Purchase history, subscription status, and payment information
  • Privacy Policy: RevenueCat Privacy Policy

Mapbox

  • Purpose: To provide mapping services and location-based features
  • Data Collected: Map usage data and location information when you use map features
  • Privacy Policy: Mapbox Privacy Policy

Radio Browser API

  • Purpose: To provide radio station data and streaming information
  • Data Collected: Station listening statistics and usage data
  • Privacy Policy: Radio Browser Privacy Policy

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide Services: Stream radio stations, display maps, and manage your favorites
  • Improve Experience: Analyze usage patterns to enhance app functionality and user experience
  • Personalize Content: Show relevant radio stations based on your location and preferences
  • Process Payments: Handle premium subscriptions and billing through RevenueCat
  • Display Ads: Show relevant advertisements to free users through Google AdMob
  • Provide Support: Respond to your questions and provide technical assistance
  • Ensure Security: Protect against fraud and unauthorized access

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • Your favorites and preferences are stored locally on your device
  • Radio station data is cached locally for offline access
  • We do not upload your personal data to our servers unless necessary for app functionality

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication for premium features
  • Regular security updates and monitoring

Data Retention

  • Local Data: Stored on your device until you uninstall the app or clear app data
  • Usage Analytics: Retained for up to 2 years for service improvement
  • Subscription Data: Retained as required by law and for billing purposes

Your Rights and Choices

Location Permissions

  • You can enable or disable location access in your device settings
  • Location is only used when you explicitly grant permission
  • You can revoke location access at any time

Ad Preferences

  • Free users can upgrade to premium to remove ads
  • Ad personalization can be controlled through Google AdMob settings
  • You can reset your advertising ID in your device settings

Data Access and Deletion

  • You can view and manage your data through the app settings
  • To delete your data, uninstall the app or contact us directly
  • Premium users can manage their subscription through RevenueCat

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy and applicable laws.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy in the app
  • Sending a notification through the app
  • Updating the "Last updated" date

Legal Basis for Processing (EU Users)

If you are in the European Union, we process your data based on:

  • Consent: For location data and optional features
  • Contract: To provide the radio streaming service
  • Legitimate Interest: For app improvement and security
  • Legal Obligation: For billing and tax purposes

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect and how we use it
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

Additional Information

App Permissions

Our app requests the following permissions:

  • Internet: To stream radio stations and fetch station data
  • Location: To show nearby stations and provide location-based features
  • Foreground Service: To continue playing music when the app is in the background
  • Wake Lock: To prevent the device from sleeping during playback
  • Notifications: To show playback controls and station information

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and safety
  • With service providers who assist in app functionality (as described above)

This Privacy Policy is effective as of the date listed above and applies to all users of the Radiova application.

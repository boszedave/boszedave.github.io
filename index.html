Privacy Policy for Radiova

Introduction

Welcome to Radiova ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you understand how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our radio streaming application.

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use Radiova, a mobile application that allows you to discover and stream radio stations from around the world.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Favorites: We store your favorite radio stations locally on your device to provide personalized recommendations.
  • Preferences: Your app settings and preferences are stored locally on your device.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: We collect basic device information such as device type, operating system version, and app version to ensure compatibility and provide support.
  • Location Data: With your explicit permission, we collect your approximate location to:
    • Show nearby radio stations on the map
    • Provide location-based station recommendations
    • Display your current location on the interactive map

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect additional information:

Google AdMob

  • Purpose: To display advertisements in the free version of our app
  • Data Collected: Ad interaction data, device identifiers, and general location information
  • Privacy Policy: Google AdMob Privacy Policy

RevenueCat

  • Purpose: To process premium subscriptions and manage billing
  • Data Collected: Purchase history, subscription status, and payment information
  • Privacy Policy: RevenueCat Privacy Policy

Mapbox

  • Purpose: To provide mapping services and location-based features
  • Data Collected: Map usage data and location information when you use map features
  • Privacy Policy: Mapbox Privacy Policy

Radio Browser API

  • Purpose: To provide radio station data and streaming information
  • Data Collected: Station listening statistics and usage data
  • Privacy Policy: Radio Browser Privacy Policy

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide Services: Stream radio stations, display maps, and manage your favorites
  • Improve Experience: Analyze usage patterns to enhance app functionality and user experience
  • Personalize Content: Show relevant radio stations based on your location and preferences
  • Process Payments: Handle premium subscriptions and billing through RevenueCat
  • Display Ads: Show relevant advertisements to free users through Google AdMob
  • Provide Support: Respond to your questions and provide technical assistance
  • Ensure Security: Protect against fraud and unauthorized access

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • Your favorites and preferences are stored locally on your device
  • Radio station data is cached locally for offline access
  • We do not upload your personal data to our servers unless necessary for app functionality

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication for premium features
  • Regular security updates and monitoring

Data Retention

  • Local Data: Stored on your device until you uninstall the app or clear app data
  • Usage Analytics: Retained for up to 2 years for service improvement
  • Subscription Data: Retained as required by law and for billing purposes

Your Rights and Choices

Location Permissions

  • You can enable or disable location access in your device settings
  • Location is only used when you explicitly grant permission
  • You can revoke location access at any time

Ad Preferences

  • Free users can upgrade to premium to remove ads
  • Ad personalization can be controlled through Google AdMob settings
  • You can reset your advertising ID in your device settings

Data Access and Deletion

  • You can view and manage your data through the app settings
  • To delete your data, uninstall the app or contact us directly
  • Premium users can manage their subscription through RevenueCat

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy and applicable laws.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy in the app
  • Sending a notification through the app
  • Updating the "Last updated" date

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at:

Email: dvainsolutions@gmail.com

If you are in the European Union, we process your data based on:

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

Privacy Policy for Elf Maker

Effective date: November 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Dvain Solutions ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Elf Maker mobile application ("App").

1) Information We Collect

Images and Face Data You Choose to Upload

  • Only the photos you explicitly select are processed. These photos may contain facial features and biometric data (face data).
  • Images containing face data are prepared locally on your device and then sent via our secure proxy to our AI processing provider (OpenAI) to generate the elf transformation.
  • We do not collect or extract biometric identifiers separately from the images you provide. Face data is contained within the images you choose to upload.

Subscription Status

  • We receive non-sensitive entitlement information (e.g., active/expired) from RevenueCat. We do not receive full payment details.

Analytics and Diagnostics

  • Basic, non-identifying diagnostics (feature usage, crashes) and generic device info (model, OS version) to improve reliability.

2) How Your Images and Face Data Are Used

Face Data Collection and Use

  • Collection: Face data is collected only when you explicitly choose to upload a photo containing facial features. We do not automatically detect or collect face data from your device.
  • Purpose: Face data is used solely to generate the requested elf transformation while keeping the person's face recognizable in the transformed image.
  • On-device processing: Selected photos containing face data are resized and optimized locally on your device before transmission.
  • Cloud processing: Images containing face data are transmitted to OpenAI's API through our secure Cloudflare Worker proxy solely to generate the transformation. The transformation process requires face data to create a recognizable elf version of the person in the image.
  • Storage: Transformed images (which may contain face data) and original images are stored locally on your device for your in‑app gallery. Images are saved to your Photos library only if you tap Save to Photos.
  • Servers: We do not persist images containing face data on our servers. OpenAI processes your images transiently (see Third‑Party Services and Data Retention sections).

3) Subscriptions, “Unlimited” Access, and Fair Use

Premium subscriptions advertise “unlimited” access; unlimited generations are subject to fair use and may be throttled to prevent abuse.

4) Third‑Party Services

OpenAI (Image Processing)

  • Purpose: Generate the elf transformation you request. Images containing face data are shared with OpenAI to perform this transformation.
  • Transport: We proxy requests through our serverless Cloudflare Worker; your OpenAI key is not embedded in the app.
  • Face Data Sharing: Images containing face data are transmitted to OpenAI's servers for processing. OpenAI processes these images according to their privacy policies and data usage policies.
  • Policies: See OpenAI's Privacy Policy and API Data Usage Policies for information about how they handle face data.

RevenueCat (Subscriptions)

  • Purpose: Manage in‑app purchases and entitlements.
  • Policy: RevenueCat Privacy Policy.

Apple (App Store & Photos)

  • Payments are handled by Apple. Photos access is requested only to save images when you choose to do so.

5) Legal Bases (EEA/UK)

  • Performance of contract to provide requested transformations.
  • Legitimate interests for reliability, abuse prevention, and analytics.
  • Consent for Photos library access.

Face Data Retention

  • Device storage: Images containing face data remain on your device until you delete them from the in‑app gallery or Photos library. You have full control over when to delete these images.
  • Server processing: Images containing face data sent to OpenAI are processed transiently for the purpose of generating the transformation. According to OpenAI's API Data Usage Policies, images are not used to train OpenAI's models and are deleted after processing. We do not retain images containing face data on our servers.
  • Third-party retention: Face data shared with OpenAI is subject to OpenAI's data retention policies as described in their API Data Usage Policies. We do not control OpenAI's retention periods.
  • Entitlements/diagnostics: Retained only as long as necessary for the purposes above.

7) Children’s Privacy

ElfMaker is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your jurisdiction). Do not use the App if you are under the applicable age.

8) Your Choices

  • Manage Photos permissions in iOS Settings.
  • Delete images from the in‑app gallery and Photos at any time.
  • Cancel subscriptions via the App Store.

9) Security

We use encryption in transit, least‑privilege access, and serverless secret management. No method is 100% secure.

10) International Transfers

Processing may occur on infrastructure outside your country. We use providers with appropriate safeguards.

11) Changes

We may update this Policy. Material changes will be communicated in‑app or on our site. Continued use signifies acceptance.

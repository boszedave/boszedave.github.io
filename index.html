Introduction

Welcome to Radiova ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you understand how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our radio streaming application.

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use Radiova, a mobile application that allows you to discover and stream radio stations from around the world.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Favorites: We store your favorite radio stations locally on your device to provide personalized recommendations.
  • Preferences: Your app settings and preferences are stored locally on your device.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: We collect basic device information such as device type, operating system version, and app version to ensure compatibility and provide support.
  • Usage Data: We collect anonymous usage statistics to improve our service.
  • Location Data: With your explicit permission, we collect your approximate location to show nearby radio stations on the map.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: To display advertisements in the free version
  • RevenueCat: To process premium subscriptions
  • Mapbox: To provide mapping services
  • Radio Browser API: To provide radio station data

How We Use Your Information

  • Stream radio stations and display maps
  • Manage your favorites and preferences
  • Process premium subscriptions
  • Display relevant advertisements
  • Improve app functionality and user experience

Data Storage and Security

  • Your favorites and preferences are stored locally on your device
  • We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information
  • Data is encrypted in transit and at rest

Your Rights and Choices

  • You can enable or disable location access in your device settings
  • Free users can upgrade to premium to remove ads
  • You can manage your subscription through RevenueCat
